Dragon Ball Characters

Dragon ball Characters I will only mention important characters, major villains, and main cast. The main cast includes both heroes and villains.

  • Goku

    Goku, also known as Kakarot, is the main protagonist of the Dragon Ball series. He is a Saiyan warrior from the planet Vegeta who was sent to Earth as a baby. Goku is known for his pure-heartedness, love for fighting, and his signature move, the Kamehameha.

  • Vegeta

    Vegeta is the prince of the fallen Saiyan race and one of the most powerful warriors in the Dragon Ball universe. Initially introduced as a villain, Vegeta eventually becomes a key ally to Goku and the Z Fighters. He is known for his prideful demeanor, fierce rivalry with Goku, and his ability to transform into a Super Saiyan.

  • Bulma

    Bulma Brief is a brilliant scientist and one of the founding members of the Dragon Team. She is known for her intelligence, technological prowess, and adventurous spirit. Bulma is also the inventor of the Dragon Radar, a device used to locate the mystical Dragon Balls.

  • Chi-Chi

    Chi-Chi is the daughter of the Ox-King and the wife of Goku. She is a caring mother to Gohan and Goten and is known for her strict demeanor and traditional values. Chi-Chi is also a skilled martial artist, having been trained by her father.

  • Trunks

    Trunks is the son of Vegeta and Bulma from the future timeline. He is a skilled fighter and a member of the Dragon Team. Trunks is known for his stoic personality, sense of duty, and his ability to transform into a Super Saiyan.

  • Gohan

    Gohan is the eldest son of Goku and Chi-Chi. He is a kind-hearted and studious individual who prefers peace over fighting. However, when the need arises, Gohan can tap into his Saiyan heritage to become a powerful warrior, much like his father.

  • Piccolo

    Piccolo, originally known as Piccolo Jr., is the reincarnation of the evil Piccolo Daimao. He is a Namekian warrior who becomes a mentor to Gohan and a valuable ally to the Z Fighters. Piccolo is known for his tactical prowess, stoic demeanor, and signature attack, the Special Beam Cannon.

  • Krillin

    Krillin, also known as Kuririn in the original Japanese version, is a former monk and one of Goku's best friends. He is a skilled martial artist and a member of the Dragon Team. Krillin is known for his bravery, loyalty, and his signature move, the Destructo Disc.

  • Android 18

    Android 18, also known as Lazuli, is a former human turned into a cyborg by Dr. Gero. She is the wife of Krillin and the mother of Marron. Android 18 is known for her calm and collected demeanor, as well as her formidable combat skills.

  • Android 17

    Android 17 is the twin brother of Android 18 and another creation of Dr. Gero. He was originally a human until he was converted into an android. Android 17 is known for his rebellious nature, love for nature, and his immense power.

  • Yamcha

    Yamcha is a former desert bandit turned martial artist and a longtime friend of Goku. He is known for his laid-back personality and his signature move, the Wolf Fang Fist. Despite his initial fear of women, Yamcha develops a close relationship with Bulma.

  • Tien

    Tien Shinhan, often referred to simply as Tien, is a disciplined martial artist and a former student of the Crane School. He is known for his third eye, which grants him heightened perception, and his powerful techniques such as the Tri-Beam.

  • Majin Buu

    Majin Buu is an ancient and malevolent being created by the evil wizard Bibidi. He is one of the most powerful villains in the Dragon Ball universe and has various forms, including Innocent Buu, Super Buu, and Kid Buu. Majin Buu is known for his insatiable appetite, childlike innocence, and destructive capabilities.

  • Frieza

    Frieza is a tyrannical alien overlord and one of the most feared villains in the universe. He is known for his ruthless nature, ability to transform into multiple forms, and his role in the destruction of Planet Vegeta. Frieza's ultimate goal is to conquer the universe and achieve immortality.

  • Cell

    Cell is a bio-engineered android created by Dr. Gero using the cells of the universe's most powerful warriors. He is known for his ability to absorb other beings to increase his power and achieve his perfect form. Cell's ultimate goal is to become the strongest fighter in the universe.

  • Raditz

    Raditz is the older brother of Goku and the first antagonist introduced in the Dragon Ball Z series. He is a ruthless Saiyan warrior who works for Frieza and aims to recruit Goku to join him in conquering other planets. Raditz is known for his disdain towards his brother and his brutal methods.

  • Nappa

    Nappa is a Saiyan warrior and the partner to vegeta but died early oon the series by Vegeta.

  • Beerus

    Beerus, also known as the God of Destruction, is one of the most powerful beings in the universe. He is responsible for maintaining balance by destroying planets, and his power surpasses even that of Goku and Vegeta. Despite his destructive nature, Beerus has a fondness for Earth's cuisine and forms a unique bond with Goku.

  • Hit

    Hit is an assassin from Universe 6 and one of the most skilled fighters in the multiverse. He is known for his time-skip technique, which allows him to briefly stop time during battles. Hit's stoic demeanor and unwavering dedication to his craft make him a formidable opponent in any tournament.

  • Zamasu

    Zamasu is a Supreme Kai apprentice from Universe 10 who becomes corrupted by his disdain for mortals. He believes that only gods are worthy of existence and seeks to eradicate all mortal life. Zamasu's obsession with his vision of justice leads him to fuse with another version of himself, becoming the omnipotent entity known as Fusion Zamasu.

  • Jiren

    Jiren, also known as Jiren the Gray, is a member of the Pride Troopers from Universe 11 and one of the strongest warriors in the multiverse. He is known for his unparalleled strength, stoic demeanor, and unwavering sense of justice. Jiren's power is so immense that he poses a significant challenge even to Goku and Vegeta in their strongest forms.

  • Black Goku

    Black Goku, also known as Goku Black, is the main antagonist of the Future Trunks Saga. He is an alternate version of Goku from an alternate timeline who has been corrupted by the evil Supreme Kai, Zamasu. Black Goku possesses all of Goku's abilities and strength, but with a sinister twist, making him a dangerous adversary for the Z Fighters.
