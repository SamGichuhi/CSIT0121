About Dragon Ball

The creator of dragon ball is Akira Toriyama a famous Japanese manga artist. In the Dragon Ball universe, remarkable individuals like Goku and Vegeta journey through vast worlds and face many challenges. They fight powerful enemies and also explore questions about who they are and what they're meant to do. Alongside their friends, they learn important lessons about bravery, determination, and understanding themselves better. Their adventures teach us about friendship, never giving up, and the strength we all have inside us.
